# ddp 🧨
Dia Del Petardo 🧨

aka. Day of the Firecracker 

# Teaser
Many years ago a kid found out his school wasn't the place he was thinking. Any similarity to reality is just a coincidence. 

# Published

On [itch.io](https://morhook.itch.io/dia-del-petardo) it's published a compiled demo. 

# History

It was running year 1999, and some kids started thinking about this game. They got together and started writing stuff to make this game. Intro ideas, design scenarios, drawed scenes, named characters, etc. The project was cancelled as making an adventure game engine was outside their coding capabilities. Fast forward 24 years and one of them found his notes, drawings. He shared them with morhook and told him "digitalize this". He was so open and cryptic that morhook started searching what engines were created
on last years. Hence, this repo was born!

# Contribute

This game is made with [wintermute engine](http://dead-code.org/home/), please send PRs of ideas of new scenes, characters or code improvements. PRs welcome and never taken for granted.
